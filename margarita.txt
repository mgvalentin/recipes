The margarita is a cocktail consisting of tequila, triple sec and lime or lemon juice, often served with salt on the rim of the glass. 

The drink is served shaken with ice, blended with ice, or without ice.
 
Main alcohol: Tequila

Ingredients: 
1 1/6 oz (7 parts) Tequila
2/3 oz (4 parts) Cointreau
1/2 oz (3 parts) Lime juice

Preparation: 
Rub the rim of the glass with the lime slice to make the salt stick to it. 
Shake the other ingredients with ice, then carefully pour into the glass (taking care not to dislodge any salt). 
Garnish and serve over ice.

Served: 
On the rocks; poured over ice

Standard garnish: 
Lime slice, Salt on the rim
